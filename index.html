// CASE 02 — Urban Frame Studio · 2022

Service Brand — Lead-Gen Rebuild

Meta Ads Funnel Creative Testing
Client B · Local Services
Account Strategist
4-Month Engagement

// The Challenge

A service brand burning ad budget on broad campaigns with weak qualified-lead conversion. Previous setup had zero audience segmentation and a single creative running across the entire funnel.

// The Approach

  • Restructured the account into a proper three-stage funnel — TOF, MOF, BOF.
  • Tested 8 creative variants per stage, retiring underperformers weekly on cost-per-result.
  • Rewrote ad copy around customer language pulled from real reviews + inquiry transcripts.
  • Tightened audience targeting using lookalikes built from purchase data, not just engagers.

// The Challenge

Brand wanted reach but had been burned by inflated rate cards. Needed influencer activations priced on actual outcome math — not vibes.

// The Approach

  • Built an internal CPE & CPM calculator using past creator engagement data.
